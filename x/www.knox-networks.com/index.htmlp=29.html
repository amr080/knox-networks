Interbank Platform

Issuance and settlement of file-based digital assets

Platform allows for atomic settlement of file-based digital assets between financial institutions.

Authorizes file-based digital assets and maintains issuance limits for issuer institutions

Remits file-based digital assets, performs foreign currency exchange, and issues and exchanges short-term liabilities

Checks sanction lists (e.g. OFAC, UN, etc.) for AML checks

ACID+D, 24/7 access, near-instantaneous settlements

Checks for double-spends

Preserves the current two-tier banking system

Access to global credit and trade corridors

Interoperable with existing and emerging financial standards (e.g. ISO 20022, SWIFT, NACHA) and payment networks, including legacy and DLT systems

Addresses pain points for financial institutions, including:

  • Slow and expensive global transactions with poor interbank communication
  • Delivery versus payment realization
  • Difficulty integrating new systems with legacy systems
  • Limited bank interoperability and high manual intervention
